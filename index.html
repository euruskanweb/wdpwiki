    The Council (World)

    The council is an elected group that is akin to the real world United Nation Security Council. The council votes on important issues that effect the whole world, including the Moon, Mars, Centaura, and Murmansk.

    Elections

    The council is elected at the first day of every month, with members being able to serve an unlimited amount of terms.

    Impeachment

    A member of the council can be impeached if the rest of the council agrees to the impeachment.

    Aphrodite

    Aphrodite is the current leader of the FSSR (Fekali Soviet Socialist Republic), he first recorded appearance being in the Kingdom of Umineko and Fekalia. She is worshipped as a goddess in Hellenism, a old-world religion proposed to exist in the fictional of "Greece".

    Fekalia

    Fekalia is a country in the WDP.

    Luhansk Peoples Republic

    The Luhansk Peoples Republic, also know as the LPR was a former country in the WDP. The lands that it once occupied are now owned by Euruska, Nova Operia, Sosnovy Bor, and the EU. They were a council member and proud putin supporter before the nation was dissolved.

    The Alban Provinces

    The Alban Provinces are a set of vassal states that are owned by the Domain of Inanna. Several have existed, however only two still remain.

    Alban Green

    Alban Green is a vassal state owned by the Domain of Inanna.

    Alban Purple

    Alban Purple is a vassal state owned by the Domain of Inanna.

    Alban Blue (Absentia)

    Alban Blue was a vassal state owned by the Domain of Inanna. It was annexed by Euruska.

    Alban Neon (Absentia)

    Alban Neon was a vassal state owned by the Domain of Inanna. It was annexed by Euruska.

    Alban Red (Absentia)

    Alban Red was a vassal state owned by the Domain of Inanna. It was annexed by an unknown power during the European Civil War.

    Siberian Republic

    The Siberian Republic is a country in the WDP. It was established after the fall of the nation of Russia.

    The Workers and Peasents Red Army (WPRA)

    The Workers and Peasents Red Army (also know as the WPRA) is a supernation occupying most of Asia. They are known for colonialism, and being one of few nations run by more than one person. As of day 19, they are the biggest nation in the WDP, with Texas rivaling at second.
