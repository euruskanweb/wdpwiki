    The Council (World)

    The council is an elected group that is akin to the real world United Nation Security Council. The council votes on important issues that effect the whole world, including the Moon, Mars, Centaura, and Murmansk.

    Elections

    The council is elected at the first day of every month, with members being able to serve an unlimited amount of terms.

    Impeachment

    A member of the council can be impeached if the rest of the council agrees to the impeachment.
